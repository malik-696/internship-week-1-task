About SPAP

Built by people who were tired of report cards arriving too late to matter

SPAP started as a classroom spreadsheet that grew up. Today it's a lightweight analytics layer schools use to turn raw scores into a running conversation about student growth.

Our approach

Three principles guide every feature

01

Show, don't archive

Data that only lives in a filing cabinet can't help anyone. Every score surfaces somewhere a teacher or student will actually look.

02

Context over raw numbers

An 82% means little alone. Trend lines and class rank give every score a story.

03

Private by default

This prototype keeps everything in your own browser's local storage — nothing is sent anywhere.

How the ledger came together

Project timeline

  • Week 1 — Structure. Static layouts for home, about, dashboard, reports, and contact.
  • Week 2 — Interaction. Working login form, validated inputs, dynamic cards, searchable tables.
  • Week 3 — Depth. Registration, password recovery, per-student profiles, sorting, and animation polish.
